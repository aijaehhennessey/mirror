I think the internet has made us simultaneously very comfortable and very uncomfortable with the concept of being known or seen as vulnerable individuals.

Sometimes the concept of being vulnerable to new people makes me feel like a clown. Acknowledging this simple fact is fun and given the amount of memes conveying this sentiment , it isn't just a me thing.

For this reason , I chose to create a simple reflection of our most-loving clown selves,just to see that characteristic reflected back physically. By acknowledging our occasional clown behavior , hopefully, we are able to learn from it.
